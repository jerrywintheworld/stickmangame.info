About Stickman Count Master

Stickman Count Master is an exciting puzzle game that challenges your counting skills and reflexes. As a stickman character, you'll navigate through various levels filled with obstacles and challenges where counting accurately is key to success.

The game features simple yet addictive mechanics that are easy to pick up but difficult to master. With colorful graphics, smooth animations, and increasingly challenging levels, Stickman Count Master provides hours of entertainment for players of all ages.

Developed by top game designers, Stickman Count Master has become one of the most popular stickman games online, with millions of players worldwide. The game is regularly updated with new levels and features to keep the gameplay fresh and exciting.

How to Play Stickman Count Master

Master the game with these simple tips and instructions

1

Objective

Your goal is to count the number of stickmen, obstacles or objects accurately in each level. Correct counting allows you to progress to more challenging stages.

2

Controls

Use your mouse to click on the correct number or drag your stickman character. On mobile devices, simply tap on the screen to interact.

3

Scoring

Earn points for each correct answer. Your score is based on accuracy and speed. Chain correct answers together to earn bonus points.

4

Power-ups

Collect power-ups that appear during gameplay to slow down time, eliminate wrong options, or double your points for a limited time.
