About Stickman WW2

Stickman WW2 brings the intensity of World War II battles to life with simple stickman graphics but deep strategic gameplay. Command your troops through historically inspired campaigns and make tactical decisions that will determine the outcome of each battle.

The game combines fast-paced action with strategic elements, requiring players to manage resources, position troops effectively, and choose the right weapons for each combat situation. Despite its simple visual style, Stickman WW2 offers surprisingly deep and engaging gameplay.

With over 2 million players worldwide, Stickman WW2 has become one of the most popular stickman war games. Regular updates introduce new campaigns, weapons, and gameplay features to keep the experience fresh and exciting.

Don't overspend on elite units early in missions. Save resources for critical moments.
