How to Master the Game

Follow these tips to become a Stickman Destruction 3 Heroes

1

Objective

Your goal is to count the number of stickmen, obstacles or objects accurately in each level. Correct counting allows you to progress to more challenging stages.

2

Controls

Use your mouse to click on the correct number or drag your stickman character. On mobile devices, simply tap on the screen to interact.

3

Scoring

Earn points for each correct answer. Your score is based on accuracy and speed. Chain correct answers together to earn bonus points.

4

Power-ups

Collect power-ups that appear during gameplay to slow down time, eliminate wrong options, or double your points for a limited time.

5

Pro Tips

Focus on patterns rather than counting individually. Use the environment to your advantage. Save power-ups for difficult levels. Practice daily to improve your reaction time.
