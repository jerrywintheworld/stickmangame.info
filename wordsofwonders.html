About Words Of Wonders

Words Of Wonders is a captivating word puzzle game that challenges players to find hidden words in a grid of letters. With hundreds of levels set in beautiful locations around the world, this game combines vocabulary building with engaging gameplay.

The game takes players on a linguistic journey through famous wonders of the world, from the Pyramids of Giza to the Great Wall of China. Each level presents a unique crossword puzzle where players must connect letters to form words that match the given clues.

Words Of Wonders stands out with its educational value, beautiful design, and progressively challenging puzzles. Whether you're a word game enthusiast or just looking to expand your vocabulary, this game offers a rewarding experience that stimulates your mind while providing hours of entertainment.

Tips & Strategies

Game Controls

  • Mouse/Touch Select letters to form words
  • Backspace Clear your current selection
  • H Get a hint (costs coins)
  • Space Shuffle letters (when available)

Game Interface

  • Clue List Shows words to find in current puzzle
  • Coin Counter Earn coins by completing puzzles
  • Hint Button Reveals a letter when stuck
  • Shuffle Rearrange letters for a fresh perspective

Word Finding Strategies

  • • Start with the longest words first - they're often easier to spot
  • • Look for common prefixes and suffixes (un-, re-, -ing, -ed)
  • • Focus on unusual letter combinations (like "qu" or "x")
  • • Remember words can be vertical, horizontal, or diagonal
  • • Take breaks - fresh eyes often spot words you previously missed
