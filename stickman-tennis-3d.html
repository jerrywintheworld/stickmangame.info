Basic Shots

S

Serve

Start the point with a powerful serve. Aim for the corners to make it difficult for your opponent to return.

F

Forehand

Your most powerful groundstroke. Hit the ball on your dominant side with a full swing for maximum power.

B

Backhand

Use one or two hands to hit the ball on your non-dominant side. Great for defensive plays.

Advanced Shots

V

Volley

Hit the ball before it bounces, usually near the net. Perfect for finishing points quickly.

SM

Smash

Overhead shot to put away high balls. Your most powerful offensive weapon.

L

Lob

Hit the ball high and deep over your opponent's head when they're at the net.

Pro Tip: Master Timing

Success in Stickman Tennis 3D depends on perfect timing. Press the shot button just as the ball reaches your player for maximum power and accuracy. Watch the ball closely and position your player correctly to hit winners!

About Stickman Tennis 3D

Stickman Tennis 3D is a thrilling sports game that brings tennis to life with simple stickman graphics but incredibly engaging gameplay. Play against challenging AI opponents or with friends in multiplayer mode. Master your serves, volleys, and smashes to become the stickman tennis champion!
